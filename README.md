# 📊 Protein Database Information

## Myosin (1B7T)

Myosin belongs to a family of ATP-dependent motor proteins responsible for converting chemical energy into mechanical movement.

Important biological roles include

- Muscle contraction
- Cellular transport
- Cell division
- Cytoskeleton organization
- Intracellular cargo movement

The crystal structure (PDB: 1B7T) captures Myosin bound with ADP and metal ions, providing valuable insights into nucleotide binding and conformational changes.
